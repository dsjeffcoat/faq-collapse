Frequently Asked Questions

Why is your name Jaysen Dante?

One day I was working on coming up with my pseudonym for my writing and just started with the simple approach of playing with my birth given name. Eventually I flipped my entire name around and that's how I played into making it Jaysen Dante.

When did you get into programming?

I've always dabbled with web development and programming since my high school years, but it wasn't until 2015 when I started taking it seriously in order to build my personal branding as a freelancer.

Why did you choose to change careers?

I noticed during my previous job as a proposal writer and even prior to that while I was going for my Masters that we are currently going through a new evolution. A digital evolution in which almost everything that we consume and gather information-wise would be digitally. Because of this, I felt that I needed to make the transition and broaden my skills to include tech skills. This eventually made me fall in love with programming.

What is your end goal?

I would love to see myself working for some of the top companies out there and helping build solutions with their brands, but I also want to build solutions within the media space to help build it into a stronger platform for everyone as well as maintaining the ethics that a lot of media professionals vow to follow during their careers.
